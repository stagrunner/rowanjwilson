About

Hey there! I'm Rowan (AKA Stag!), a digital artist, content creator, and community/social media manager in the Central New York area. I'm a grizzled ancient of the internet age and boast nearly a decade of experience in the creation/curation of online communities (and their social media!). I specialize in building presences in the gaming space and take great pride in assisting in the creation of progressive, LGBT-centric spaces.

In addition to my work in community & social media management, I boast a fondness for digital art and web design. Though I've chosen to focus his career on the people *behind* web sites rather than the sites themselves, I've not forgotten my NeoPets routes & still enjoy making time to tinker and create online.
